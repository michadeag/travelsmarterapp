📋 Week-Before Checklist

Status Matching

Check current elite status on your frequent flyer accounts
Research matching programs for your target airline
Prepare documentation (credit card statements, loyalty cards)
Submit status match request 7-10 days before travel

Hotel & Booking Prep

Book hotels through loyalty programs (not OTAs)
Request room upgrade at check-in for elite members
Add email address to hotel system for communication
Screenshot booking confirmation with loyalty number

Travel Logistics

Check award seat availability 11 months in advance
Book flexible tickets (refundable or changeable)
Add Global Entry/TSA PreCheck to airline profile
Check baggage allowance and seat assignment options

✈️ At-Airport Checklist

Check-In

Check in online 24 hours before departure
Choose best seat available (extra legroom if elite)
Have loyalty card ready for agent to see
Ask agent about elite benefits and upgrades

Gate Area

Verify boarding group and seat assignment on phone
Look for seat upgrade opportunities on screens
Check elite lounge access with boarding pass
Ask gate agent about meal/beverage service class

Boarding

Board in elite group when called
Introduce yourself to flight crew and acknowledge elite status
Ask about amenity kit or upgrades after boarding
Take photo of seat/amenities for community sharing
