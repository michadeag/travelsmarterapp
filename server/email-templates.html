💡 Pro Tips for Email Success

📝 Personalize Everything

Add specific details like dates, loyalty numbers, and your achievements. Generic emails get ignored.

⏰ Timing Matters

Send during business hours (9am-5pm in their timezone). Monday-Thursday get better response rates.

✨ Keep It Professional

Use proper spelling and grammar. Sign with your full name. Make it easy for them to respond.

📞 Follow Up

If no response in 5 business days, send a polite follow-up. Many reps miss emails and appreciate the reminder.

🎯 One Ask Per Email

Don't ask multiple things in one email. Focus on one clear request to increase success rates.

📱 Include Contact Info

Put your phone number and email at the end. Make it easy for them to respond however they prefer.
